## Gym Facilities

Cobra Boxing and Fitness offers a practical training space built for all experience levels, from first-time gym users to regular athletes.

### Training Areas

- Free weights area for strength and conditioning
- Resistance machine stations for guided training
- Cardio area for endurance and fitness work
- Boxing-focused space for pad work, drills, and conditioning

### Equipment Highlights

- Dumbbells, barbells, and benches
- Cable and selectorized machines
- Cardio machines for warm-up and interval work
- Bags and functional training equipment

### Member Amenities

- Friendly coaching support when needed
- Flexible access options through day and monthly passes
- Inclusive environment for adults, juniors, and families

### Need Help Getting Started?

If you are new to the gym floor, speak to the team and we will help you choose the right starting plan.
