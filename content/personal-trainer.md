## Personal Training

Build strength, fitness, and confidence with one-to-one coaching tailored to your goals, schedule, and current fitness level.

### What You Get

- Goal-focused sessions designed around your experience level
- Technique coaching for boxing fundamentals and conditioning
- Structured progress plans to keep you improving week by week
- Support with routine, accountability, and consistency

### Personal Training Packages

#### Intro Session

- 60-minute one-to-one session
- Movement and fitness baseline check
- First month action plan

#### Weekly Coaching

- One to three sessions per week
- Progressive training plan with regular check-ins
- Flexible focus: fat loss, fitness, strength, or boxing skills

#### Performance Focus

- Boxing-specific conditioning and pad work
- Speed, stamina, and power development
- Technique refinement for drills and sparring prep

### Who It Is For

- Complete beginners who want expert guidance from day one
- Busy adults who need focused sessions that use time efficiently
- Members returning after a break who want a safe restart plan
- Boxers looking to sharpen technique and improve conditioning

### Why Train One-to-One at Cobra

- Coaching tailored to your ability, injuries, and goals
- Private, focused sessions with clear progression
- A supportive gym environment that keeps you motivated
