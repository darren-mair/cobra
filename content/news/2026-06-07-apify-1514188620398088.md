An exciting opportunity is available at Cobra Boxing & Fitness in our main boxing room. 

Our room is now available to rent for hourly slots throughout the day.
You will have exclusive use of the room however must adhere to the following rules:

• Respect our property👍
• No kicking the bags❌
• Leave the room the way you find it👍
• No contact sparring at all❌

The room has everything you need to put on all kinds of classes and you will have access to the following:

• Large bluetooth speaker🎼
• Large cooling fans🥵
• 6 x punch bags💥🥊
• Assault bike🚴🏻‍♂️
• Ski Erg😫💦

• Weight Dumbells💪
