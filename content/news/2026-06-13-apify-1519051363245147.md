We are back today with our Kids Class

A hear parents saying I am not taking my kids to boxing or I don’t want my bairn getting punched. That’s not what we do and there is so much more to boxing like:

• Fitness training
• Having fun
• Staying active
• Gaining confidence
• Making new friends
• Learning life skills

So many more reasons but even from a young age children learn to follow instructions, have structure, give and earn respect whilst also having fun.

Give it a try bring your son or daughter down and you might be surprised.

Every one of these kids came for the 1st time and never looked back💥🥊
