💥🥊Cobra Boxing & Fitness Need Your Support💥🥊

As our gym continues to grow and more children walk through our doors each week, our equipment gets a lot more use. 

While this is fantastic to see it also means some of our boxing gloves have become worn and smelly and need replaced.🙈

At Cobra Boxing & Fitness we are very proud to provide a safe, positive enviroment where children can build confidence, discipline, respect and self belief. 
We work extremely hard to keep kids active, off the streets and focused on achieving their goals both inside and outside the gym. 

We are reaching out to anyone that maybe able to support us by sponsorships, donating or contributions towards new equipment. If you would like to support us with the work we are doing in the community then please get in touch. 

Together we can continue to provide opportunities for local children and young people to learn valuable life skills through boxing. 

Thankyou to everyone that has already supported Cobra Boxing & Fitness - we could not do it without you.❤️
